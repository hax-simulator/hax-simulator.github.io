Interface InboundDevice

  • All Superinterfaces:
    Device
    All Known Subinterfaces:
    DuplexDevice, Keyboard, NetworkInterface

    public interface InboundDevice
extends Device
    A device capable of receiving data and notifying a registered listener. The listener is the representation of a program waiting for input from the device.
